Case Study : Indian Debit Card fraud 2016

  1. ATMs exposed to malware
  2. PIN Code exposed : Skimming or Vishing
    1. What is Skimming?

      Illegally collect data from the magnetic stripe of a credit, debit or ATM card without knowledge of cardholder.

    2. What is Vishing? (Voice + Phishing)

      The illegal access of data via voice over Internet Protocol (VoIP).
